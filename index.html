1.CMR Institute of Technology, Hyderabad

CMR Institute of Technology, Hyderabad is a premier engineering college known for its academic excellence and state-of-the-art facilities. Established in 2005, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.
